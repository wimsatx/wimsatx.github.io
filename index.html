Empowering Excellence: Women in Mathematics Workshop in San Antonio


September 13-15, 2024
University of Texas at San Antonio


** Applications for the workshop are FREE and OPEN.
Although the priority deadline has passed, you can still register here. Funding support is subject to availability. **

Our career workshop offers an exciting opportunity for undergraduate and graduate female students to explore various career paths and opportunities in mathematics, where you can
* hear from esteemed speakers in academia and research labs about different career pathways in mathematical fields
* receive tips on graduate school applications and summer research opportunities
* showcase your own projects through poster presentations in a supportive environment and compete for a poster award
* experience a professional conference firsthand with our funding support
* build connections and expand your professional network with established mathematicians and like-minded peer

Additionally, we are offering a pre-workshop poster tutorial in mid-July to help you prepare for the poster presentation.  More details about the poster tutorial can be found on the poster page.

We welcome applications from all undergraduate and graduate students in the greater San Antonio area, majoring in math-related fields. Priority will be given to female and underrepresented students.

Inclusivity

We are committed to promoting equality, diversity, and inclusion for all participants. Any violations of the workshop's non-discrimination policy should be reported to the organizers. By upholding these principles, the workshop can foster an empowering experience for everyone. Should you encounter any situation during the conference that makes you feel unwelcome, we encourage you to reach out to one of the organizers, who will assist you to the best of our ability.
